Welcome to your brief respite, adventurer.
You and your companions have travelled far - why not play a game of dice after making camp?

The rules are simple.
The game will go on for three rounds. During each round, you and your opponent will both roll a pair of dice.
At the end of the three rounds, the player with the most points win.

Here are how points for each round are calculated:

  1. If a player rolls a 1 on either of their dice, they score 0 for the round.
  2. If a player rolls a pair of the same numbers, they score the sum of both dice multiplied by two.
  3. Otherwise, a player will score the sum of their two dice.
To begin, press the "Roll Dice" button. Press the button again to advance to the next round.
To start a new game, press the "New Game" button.

Good luck!
